## 💡 Pro Tips for Image Capture

### Make Them Professional
1. **Use Clean Interfaces** - No clutter, no personal data
2. **Good Composition** - Center key features
3. **Consistent Style** - All images look like they belong together
4. **High Quality** - 1920x1080px minimum for detail images
5. **Optimized Size** - Use TinyPNG, keep under limits

### Privacy First
- ❌ NO real names or personal data
- ❌ NO unblurred faces (especially KhunCaddy!)
- ✅ Use sample/demo data
- ✅ Blur sensitive information
- ✅ Use staging environments
